About Me

I am a researcher in AMO (Atomic, Molecular, and Optical) Physics. My PhD focused on dipolar quantum gases of erbium atoms, exploring superradiance across the BEC-droplet transition. I have experience in quantum simulations using the extended Gross-Pitaevskii equation (eGPE) and have worked on quantum spin chains.

I earned my PhD in Physics from HKUST and a BS-MS dual degree from IISER Kolkata. My research interests include quantum gases, nonlinear dynamics, and precision measurement physics.
